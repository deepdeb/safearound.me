About Us

Our goal is to make the world a better and a healthy place to live in.

How do we do that?

We use reliable user data to map points in an area where it's unsafe to go to.

A personalized user map tells you where you could go and stay safe and healthy! Ours is a team dedicated to producing the best user-experience and giving you the best value for contributing to the map.
